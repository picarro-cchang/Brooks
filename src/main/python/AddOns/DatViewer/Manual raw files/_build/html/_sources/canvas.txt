.. _mouse_events:

Mouse motion and figure transform
==================================

* Left click and drag: zoom in  

* Left click with SHIFT key down: shift  

* Left click with CTRL key down: zoom out

* Left click with ALT key down: stretch

* Right click: righ-key menu
